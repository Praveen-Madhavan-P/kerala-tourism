Parampikulam Wild life Sanctuary

Spread over an area of nearly 400 square meters, the wildlife sanctuary is one of the most famous Palakkad tourist places for its Wild Tiger Reserve.Wildlife enthusiasts and thrill seekers will find it perfect to spend a day among the plethora of flora and fauna


Kanjirapuzha Dam

Nestled 13 kms from Mannarkkad is the little hamlet of Kanjipurzha famous among tourists for the majestic dam of the same name. With a catchment area of over 7000 hectares, the dam is an idyllic getaway from the hustle and bustle of everyday city life.
